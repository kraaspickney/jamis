Going Shopping?

Comparative shopping is the most basic and, yet, vitally important strategy employed by today’s smart buyer. It can be a shopper’s market if shopping savvy is used – of which knowledge (information, facts and figures) is an integral part.

JAMIS encourages smart shopping by placing the Virtual Shopping Basket at your very fingertips. Simply follow the next two (2) steps which include selecting from known favourite shopping locations within the Corporate Area, along with specifying the commodities in which you may have an interest in purchasing.

The Virtual Shopping Basket, then, will generate a comparative shopping report which allows you to make informed shopping decisions from your own home.
